// CHARACTER BIO

About Me

KIRA VOSS
CLASS: Game Director
LVL 42
HP
MP
STM

I've been making games since I was 14 and accidentally shipped a text adventure to 200 people on a BBS. That game had three endings, two of which were bugs. I called them "features."

Seventeen years later, I've directed titles across survival horror, open-world action, narrative RPGs, and one deeply cursed farming simulator that somehow won an award. I believe great games live in the margins — in the weird animation that plays when you pet the dog, in the flavor text nobody reads (I read it), in the secret room behind the waterfall.

When I'm not shipping games, I'm dissecting them. I've given talks at GDC, IndieCade, and one very confused TED audience who thought I was there to talk about productivity. (I was — game design is productivity. Fight me.)

Currently looking for the next great project. If your game needs a director who will obsess over your jump feel for three weeks straight, let's talk.
