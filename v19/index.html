The best design is invisible.

It earns trust by making the right choice obvious.

I build systems, not just features. My work spans combat design, open-world systems, progression architectures, and team leadership across AAA studios.

Every system I design starts with one question: does this make the player's experience better? If a design needs a tutorial to explain itself, it needs more work.
